Syntetica Trax

Founded in 2023 by André Dias and Cardao, Syntetica Trax is a techno label based in Spain that is dedicated to preserving the old grooves of techno that are often lost in today's scene. The label focuses on deep, driving, emotive and soulful techno that creates a unique and groovy atmosphere, inviting people from all walks of life to come together and lose themselves in the music while expressing themselves freely. The label's culture is one of inclusivity and diversity, where everyone is welcome and encouraged to be their true selves. The label's sound, atmosphere, and community all come together to create an unforgettable and transformative experience for all who are a part of it.
